Welcome to Kill Your Friends, the friendly assassin game!

You will be assigned a target, and it is your task to kill that person.

You will achieve this with the deadliest of weapons: words

When the game begins, you will be given a unique secret weapon. If your target says this word, they are dead! You must tell them this so that they can record it on their device.

An example:

Simon is trying to kill Chris by getting him to say the word "pasta"

SIMON: Hey Chris, I was thinking, what is your favourite food?

CHRIS: I would say my favourite food is probably pasta

SIMON: You're DEAD! The word was "pasta."

CHRIS: Oh no! I'll have to push the button now to let the game know

Please don't reveal your word to other players, or contract other players to do your killing for you

If your victim says their word by random chance, you MAY declare them dead, but we have tried to pick words where this won't happen

Try to convince your victim to say the word without saying it yourself.

Another example:

Simon is trying to kill Chris by getting him to say the word "pasta"

SIMON: Hey Chris, what is your favourite kind of pasta?

CHRIS: I would say my favourite pasta is probably spaghetti

SIMON: You're DEAD! The word was "pasta."

CHRIS: No, that doesn't count! And now I know the word I'm INVINCIBLE

SIMON: Oh no! :(

If this happens, you can ask to be assigned a new word but it will cost you a point.
